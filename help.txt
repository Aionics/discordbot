EZbot 0.9 command list:


music:
    !joinVoice <channelId> - join bot to voice channel with specific id (if you want to find id - than go to settings and enable developer mode, than you can right click on the channel to copy its id)
    !summon - join bot to voice channel you are currently in
    //!play <audionName> - play audio to the voice channel
    //!playlist - show list of added audiofiles
    !tts "text to speech"
    !stop - stopping current audio
    !setvol <volume> - set music volume (from 0 to 1, 0.3 - default)

other:
    !status - get bot's status
    !help - command to get this list
